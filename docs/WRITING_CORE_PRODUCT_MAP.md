# Writing-Core Product Map

## Muc tieu

Tam thoi bo qua Lab va cac man roadmap chua hoan thien.
StoryForge nen duoc nhin nhu mot workspace viet truyen co 1 flow chinh, thay vi nhieu cum tinh nang song song.

## So do product de xuat

1. Dashboard
   - Chon project
   - Tao project moi
   - Mo lai du an dang viet

2. Story Setup
   - Story Bible
   - Character Hub
   - World Lore
   - Canon Truth

3. Story Planning
   - Outline Board
   - Arc / chapter structure
   - Plot thread co ban

4. Story Writing
   - Scene Editor
   - AI Sidebar
   - Scene contract
   - Continuity support

5. Story Control
   - Canon review
   - Project settings
   - Story creation settings

## Quy tac product surface

- Chi de lo nhung man ho tro truc tiep cho flow viet truyen.
- Lab, Corpus, Analysis Viewer, Timeline, Revision QA, Style Lab khong dua vao navigation chinh.
- Canon Truth khong dong vai tro mot hub chung nua; no la man kiem soat canon sau khi viet.

## Navigation rut gon

- Dashboard
- So tay truyen
- Su that
- Outline Board
- Nhan vat
- The gioi
- Viet truyen
- Cai dat khi tao truyen
- Cai dat

## Nguyen tac thiet ke

- Editor la trung tam.
- Story Bible la noi dinh nghia.
- Outline la noi lap ke hoach.
- Canon Truth la noi kiem tra va khoa su that.
- Moi tinh nang moi trong tuong lai phai map duoc vao 1 trong 4 nhom: Setup, Planning, Writing, Control.
